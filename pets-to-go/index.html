Mission & Vision

Pets to Go is a non-profit no-kill rescue whose mission is to rescue, protect, and provide hope as we search for loving homes for stray and neglected animals. We promote animal welfare, strengthen the human-animal bond, and prevent the overpopulation of pets. We provide hope when they have given up hope, we speak when they cannot speak, and we love without boundaries.

Pet Helpline

Pets To Go is dedicated to keeping pets out of shelters and rescues and helping them remain in homes with people who love them. This is why we have a Pet Helpline to assist pet owners who are struggling to resolve issues that could result in them leaving their pet at the shelter.

Upcoming Events

Adopt-a-Rama

Saturday, January 9th 10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m

The Annual Adopt-a-Rama adoption event is free to attend. The only thing required of you is an open heart and a willingness to give a homeless pet a second chance. If you happen to meet your four-legged soulmate, we ask that you have a valid ID and be ready to pay the adoption fee. If you rent your home, a letter from your landlord giving you permission to have a pet will help ensure the adoption goes smoothly.

Obedience Class 101

Tuesday, January 9th 6:30 p.m. to 7:30 p.m. You must be enrolled to attend.

This class is for dogs 5 months and older. Guardians will learn how to teach their dogs in a humane manner using positive reinforcement methods which in turn will enrich their relationship with their dog. Classes consist of 4 weekly sessions, each lasting about 50 minutes. Each class will meet on the same schedule day and time once a week for 4 weeks.
